About

Welcome to JournalRing, a webring for people who, well...like to journal! This layout is based on a Hobonichi Techo planner, but you don't have to use any specific brand to qualify. As long as you enjoy writing things down in a notebook or journal and have a website, you're eligible to join. Head over to the "Join" tab for a list of rules and a sign-up form, or check out the "Members" tab to view our roster.

Rules

  1. You must enjoy journalling, whether you're a bujo influencer with a prolific arsenal of fountain pens or someone who just scribbles in a random notebook you found.
  2. You have to run a website to join JournalRing. It can be hosted wherever you like, but you need somewhere to actually post the code.
  3. Websites will be periodically scanned for dead links. If your site is taken down, you'll be removed from the webring. If you ever return, feel free to re-apply.
  4. No websites that are specifically dedicated to NSFW content will be accepted. This webring is not currently age restricted, so keep this in mind before submitting your site.
  5. All sites must follow US law. Nothing illegal!
  6. These rules are all subject to change if necessary.

Widgets

Once you've applied, you can add a widget of your choice to your site. Before you're accepted, the widget will display a pending notice. If you want to override any CSS, you can do so by targeting classes listed in the JS.
